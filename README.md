# floofplayer - Local Video Player

this is a simple custom video player built using just **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**, its designed to run a video in your web browser.

---

## Features

* **Dark Mode Only**
* **Smooth Controls**
* **Video Scrubbing**, click and drag the timeline bar to seek.
* **Speed Control**, jump between 0.5x, 1x, 2x, and 4x playback speeds.
* **Stop Button**, pauses and resets the video to 0, and lets you **pick a new file**.
* **Filters**, apply weird screen filters like Noir or Ghost.
* **Rotation** and **Frame Stepping**.
* **Ambience**, background glow that tries to match the video colors (may torture your pc).
* **Keyboard Shortcuts** for fast control (space/k to toggle play/pause, arrows for seeking/volume).
* **"Tech Junk" Log**, an optional console output. please, just sniff me

---

## How to Use

1.  **Load a video**, click the **Browse Files** button in the center of the video player.
2.  **Pick your video**, select any video file from your pc (mp4, webm, etc.).
3.  **Enjoy**, the video will start playing.
